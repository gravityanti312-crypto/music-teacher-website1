Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your first lesson.

Still have questions? Give me a call anytime at 480-292-4799 — I'd love to chat.

What ages do you teach?

I teach students from age 5 through adult — there's truly no upper limit. Many of my most rewarding students started in their 40s, 50s, and beyond.

Do I need to own an instrument?

Yes — students will need access to an instrument at home to practice. I'm always happy to advise on what to buy or rent for your level and budget.

How long are lessons?

Most students do 30-minute weekly lessons to start, moving to 45 or 60 minutes as they advance. We'll choose what fits your goals.

What if my child has never played before?

Perfect — that's my favorite place to start. Absolute beginners get a structured, joyful introduction so they fall in love with music from day one.

Are recitals required?

Never required, but always available. Recitals are a wonderful way to celebrate progress, but I respect every student's comfort level.
